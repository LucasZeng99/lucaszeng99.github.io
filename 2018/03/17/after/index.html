2018-03-17

Contents

my team ranks 13th out of 21, and we solved 4 questions out of 12.

what I learned

  1. the importance of pseudocode

    the more I wrote pseudocode on the draft paper, the more I was concentraded on the codes and it forced me to finish the logic first instead of doing it while coding. And that can leave me away from the basic setup and other distractions.

  2. communication skill

    during the contest, three of us chose the questions to solve individually. While debugging, however, each author has to be able to explain what they are doing, and every audience or debugger has to pay full attention to understand.

    At the time, I was tired, and could not be able to comprehend what my friend is talking about.
